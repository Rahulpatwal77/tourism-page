Explore Beautiful Places of Uttarakhand

Nainital

Picturesque lake town surrounded by emerald hills and a peaceful vibe.

Mussoorie

Charming hill station with scenic views and colonial heritage.

Rishikesh

Yoga capital of the world, known for adventure sports and spiritual energy.

Haridwar

Holy city on the banks of the Ganges, famous for evening Ganga Aarti.

Kedarnath

One of the Char Dhams, nestled in the Himalayas, devoted to Lord Shiva.

Badrinath

Sacred pilgrimage site and temple of Lord Vishnu in majestic surroundings.

Chopta

Mini Switzerland of India, gateway to Tungnath and Chandrashila trek.

Valley of Flowers

UNESCO World Heritage site with vibrant alpine flora and scenic treks.

Jim Corbett

India's oldest national park, home to the Bengal tiger and rich wildlife.

Tungnath

Highest Shiva temple in the world, located in serene Garhwal hills.

Tehri Lake

Artificial reservoir offering boating and water adventure sports.

Uttarkashi

Spiritual and trekking base town near Gangotri and Yamunotri routes.

Surkanda Devi

Sacred temple dedicated to Goddess Durga, offering panoramic views and peaceful vibes.
