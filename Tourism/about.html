About DIVINORA

Welcome to DIVINORA – your digital travel companion to the divine land of Uttarakhand! Known as the “Land of Gods” (Devbhoomi), Uttarakhand offers majestic mountains, serene rivers, spiritual sites, and unexplored natural beauty.

Our tourism app helps you explore popular destinations like Mussoorie, Nainital, Rishikesh, Kedarnath, Badrinath and hidden gems across the state. We provide curated guides, local insights, scenic photos, and travel tips to make your journey memorable and comfortable.

Whether you seek spiritual peace, adventurous trekking, or nature’s bliss – DIVINORA is your gateway to the soul of Uttarakhand.
